# Research Paper Series on AI Agents

## Goal
I want to write a series of research papers to help readers better understand AI agents and how it can be used to build advanced AI systems that can perform complex tasks with minimal human intervention.

## Series Overview
### Series #1: Understanding AI Agents and Autonomy
**Goal:** Define AI agents.
**Objectives:**
- Understand what are AI agents and their components
- Clarify ambiguous terms such as "Agentic AI", "Intelligent Agents", "Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)", etc.
- Understand autonomy in AI agents and its different levels
- Understand the correlation between various level of autonomy in AI agents, level of user involvement, level of risk (which translates to the amount of oversight and governance needed).
- Understand what ReAct framework is and how it can be used to develop advanced AI agents

### Series #2: How Well-defined API Contracts Enable Tools for AI Agents
**Goal:** Explain the importance of well-defined API contracts for AI agents.
**Objectives:**
- Understand what are API contracts
- Understand what are tools for AI agents
- Understand what are Model Context Protocols (MCP) in relation to tools for AI agents
- Learn how well-defined API contracts can enable tools for AI agents

### Series #3: Securing AI Agents
**Goal:** Discuss security considerations for AI agents.
**Objectives:**
- Understand potential security risks associated with AI agents
- Learn best practices for securing AI agents
- Explore case studies of security breaches involving AI agents and lessons learned
- Understand regulatory and compliance considerations for AI agents
- Learn about emerging security technologies and techniques for AI agents
- Understand the role of human oversight in ensuring the security of AI agents
- Explore future trends and challenges in securing AI agents
- Provide actionable recommendations for organizations looking to implement secure AI agents

### Series #4: How to Choose the Right AI Models for Your AI Agents
**Goal:** Guide readers on selecting appropriate AI models for their AI agents.
**Objectives:**
- Understand different types of AI models available for AI agents: large language models, narrow language models, small language models
- Learn criteria for selecting AI models based on use case requirements
- Explore case studies of successful AI agent implementations with different AI models
- Understand trade-offs between model size, performance, and resource requirements
- Provide practical tips and best practices for choosing the right AI models for AI agents
- Discuss future trends in AI model development and their implications for AI agents
- Offer actionable recommendations for organizations looking to implement AI agents with the right AI models

### Series #5: AI Use Case Canvas
**Goal:** Evaluate AI use cases.
**Objectives:**
- Understand what is AI Use Case Canvas
- Learn how to use AI Use Case Canvas to identify and evaluate potential AI use cases
- Learn how to prioritize AI use cases based on business value and feasibility

### Series #6: Deep Dive into Financial Use Cases
**Goal:** Highlight practical and high-value busines scenarios in the financial industry where AI agents can drive measurable business impact.
**Objectives:**
- Explore specific financial use cases such as business process management, risk management, knowledge management, Agent-to-Agent (A2A) customer engagement.
- Discuss implementation strategies, challenges, and best practices for deploying AI agents in these scenarios.
- Showcase real-world examples and case studies to illustrate the benefits and outcomes of using AI agents in financial services.
- Provide actionable insights and recommendations for financial institutions looking to leverage AI agents to enhance their operations and customer experiences.

### Series #7: Deep Dive into Organization-specific AI Platforms (i.e., AI Factory)
**Goal:** Explore how various lines-of-businesses (LOBs) in the organizations can build their AI agents using the organization's own AI platforms.
**Objectives:**
- Understand the architecture and components of organization-specific AI platforms (e.g., AI Factory).
- Learn how LOBs can leverage these platforms to develop, deploy, and manage AI agents effectively.
- Discuss best practices for integrating AI agents with existing systems and workflows within the organization.
- Showcase case studies of successful AI agent implementations using organization-specific AI platforms.
- Provide practical guidance and recommendations for LOBs looking to build and scale AI agents using their organization's AI platforms.
- Explore future trends and innovations in organization-specific AI platforms and their impact on AI agent development.
- Offer actionable strategies for organizations to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among LOBs in AI agent development.
